Hello! My name is Laura, and this is my color-coded archive.

I have long been captivated by the notion of curating a color-centric archive, essentially an assemblage of distinct entries organized by their respective hues. To bring this vision to life, I carefully selected eleven colors and embarked on the creative process of categorizing content inspired by each. The resulting archive has thus far yielded five unique entries, each imbued with its own significance.

For instance, my affinity for the color pink, a shade that consistently captures my attention, inspired me to compile a collection of photographs. These images reflect moments when I encountered the captivating allure of this color and felt compelled to immortalize them through my lens.

The color white, in my mind, evokes a sense of tranquility. To pay homage to this perception, I've incorporated some of my favorite quotes, which elegantly transform and reveal their Spanish translations upon hovering over them. This serves not only to highlight my Argentinean heritage but also offers a glimpse into the way my mind navigates between languages.

In the case of red, I've drawn from my deep-rooted admiration for Taylor Swift's "RED" album. Within this entry, you'll discover the evocative lyrics from her songs, accompanied by an audio clip of "Red" itself. This was a defining album during my formative years, and I've always associated the color red with the indelible impact of Taylor's music.

The soft embrace of light blue pays tribute to my profound love for the ocean, an affection cultivated by my upbringing on an island. It is an integral part of my life and finds representation within this collection.
